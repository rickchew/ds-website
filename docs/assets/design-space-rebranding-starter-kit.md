# Design Space Sdn Bhd Rebranding Project Starter Kit

**Project:** Rebrand Design Space Sdn Bhd  
**New Direction:** AI Product, Data & Automation Studio  
**Prepared for:** Rick Chew / Design Space Sdn Bhd  
**Date:** 16 May 2026  
**Document Purpose:** A complete Markdown starter file to guide the rebranding, website strategy, service structure, sales positioning, and internal rollout of Design Space.

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Design Space Sdn Bhd should evolve from a traditional design / website studio into a modern **AI Product, Data & Automation Studio**.

The company should no longer be positioned as a vendor that simply creates websites, logos, or mobile apps. Instead, Design Space should become a strategic digital partner that helps businesses move from manual operations to intelligent digital systems.

The new Design Space should help companies with the full digital business loop:

```text
Branding
  ↓
Website
  ↓
Lead Generation
  ↓
CRM / Automation
  ↓
Custom Web App / Mobile App
  ↓
IoT Hardware-to-Cloud
  ↓
Data Collection
  ↓
Business Intelligence
  ↓
AI / Deep Learning
  ↓
Business Growth
```

The website remains important, but it is now the **entry point**. It is the first step to attract quality customers, understand their business problems, and later engage them with custom systems, mobile apps, AI automation, IoT, and data intelligence services.

## 6. Core Business Concept: The Design Space Digital Loop

The main framework for the rebrand should be:

# The Design Space Digital Loop

```text
Brand
  ↓
Website
  ↓
Leads
  ↓
CRM
  ↓
Automation
  ↓
Custom App
  ↓
IoT
  ↓
Data
  ↓
AI Insight
  ↓
Growth
```

### 6.1 Explanation

Most businesses start by asking for a website. But a website alone does not solve business problems.

A proper digital system should:

1. Present the company professionally.
2. Generate leads and enquiries.
3. Capture customer data.
4. Automate repetitive work.
5. Support daily operations through apps or dashboards.
6. Connect physical operations through IoT when needed.
7. Collect business data.
8. Analyze patterns.
9. Use AI to recommend or automate actions.
10. Help the business grow.

This is the closed-loop value of Design Space.

## 8. Service Maturity Ladder

Many clients will not buy AI or deep learning immediately. Design Space should guide them step by step.

```text
Level 1: Brand & Website
Level 2: Lead Capture & CRM
Level 3: Automation
Level 4: Custom Web App / Mobile App
Level 5: Dashboard & Data Intelligence
Level 6: AI Assistant
Level 7: Deep Learning / Prediction / Computer Vision
Level 8: IoT + AI Closed Loop
```

### How to Explain to Clients

> You do not need to start with advanced AI. We help you build the right digital foundation first. Once your business process and data are ready, we can add automation, dashboards, AI agents, and deeper intelligence step by step.

## 9. Target Customer Profile

Design Space should not target low-budget customers who only want a cheap website.

### 9.1 Best Target Customers

- SMEs with 10–100 staff
- Companies with offline operations
- Companies with multiple outlets
- Companies using WhatsApp heavily
- Businesses with manual admin work
- Companies using Excel for daily operation
- Businesses needing customer follow-up
- Companies with machines or physical equipment
- Businesses that want an app but do not know how to start
- Companies that want AI but have no data foundation
- Companies with existing revenue but weak digital structure

### 9.2 Suitable Industries

| Industry | Why Suitable |
|---|---|
| Laundry / vending / self-service | App, payment, IoT, dashboard, machine activation |
| Marine / logistics / offshore | Corporate image, quotation, document flow, tracking, reporting |
| Property / short stay | Booking, guest automation, smart lock, CRM |
| Retail chains | Loyalty, CRM, stock, reporting, membership |
| F&B chains | Ordering, loyalty, outlet reporting, customer data |
| Education / training centers | Student portal, payment, scheduling, reminders |
| Equipment rental | Booking, asset tracking, maintenance, IoT |
| Service companies | Quotation, job assignment, customer follow-up |
| Manufacturing / workshop | Production tracking, quality detection, IoT sensors |
| Medical / wellness clinics | Appointment, CRM, reminders, patient journey |
| Automotive / used car | Inventory, CRM, inspection, booking, marketplace |
| Construction / renovation | Lead management, quotation, project tracking |

### 9.3 Avoid Customers

Avoid clients who:

- Only compare price
- Want a RM800 website
- Have no intention to improve business operations
- Do not want to provide content
- Do not value strategy
- Refuse any monthly maintenance
- Expect unlimited revisions
- Want advanced AI with no budget or data
- Have no decision-maker involved

## 37. Client Education: Website vs Web App vs Automation vs AI

### Website

A website presents your company, services, credibility and contact information.

Best for:

- Company profile
- Service explanation
- SEO
- Lead generation
- Trust building

### Web App

A web app allows users or staff to log in, manage data, perform actions, and run business processes.

Best for:

- Booking
- Admin panel
- Reporting
- Customer portal
- Staff operation

### Automation

Automation connects tasks and tools so repetitive work happens with less manual effort.

Best for:

- Follow-up
- Reminders
- Reports
- Notifications
- Customer service

### AI

AI helps understand, generate, classify, summarize, predict, or recommend based on data and instructions.

Best for:

- AI assistant
- Customer support
- Report summary
- Search company knowledge
- Prediction
- Pattern detection

### IoT

IoT connects physical devices and machines to cloud systems.

Best for:

- Remote monitoring
- Machine activation
- Sensor data
- Equipment status
- Alerts
