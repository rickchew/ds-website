### Case study template (rebrand doc §24)

Every case study page should follow:
- Background (client / industry)
- Challenge (problem)
- Goal (what the project needed to achieve)
- Solution (what Design Space built)
- Key Features
- Technology / Capability Used (mapped to: Branding · Website · Web app · Mobile app · Automation · IoT · Data · AI)
- Result (what improved)
- Future Potential (what can be added next — implicitly upsells the Digital Loop)

### myDobi — full vertical case study
The most complete piece of work in the 2022 deck. May be the same project as "Hiro" in the rebrand framing — confirm with user.
- **Brand identity** — Logo, color theme (Orange `C0 M26 Y86 K0` + Black `K100`), brand image
- **App** — myDobi Connect: "first laundry brand in Malaysia with self-service mobile app + e-wallet". Modules: Loyalty, E-wallet, IoT, Dealer Management, Analysis Report, Store Locator, Outlets Management, Issue Tracker
- **Interior fitout** — wall design, color theme, instruction posters for laundromat outlets (Piasau outlet shown)
- **Product line packaging** — Double Wash (35 Min), Power Clean detergent, Strong Protection sanitizer, Super Soft softener — bespoke product logos and posters
- **Container Laundry Design Concept 2022** — shipping-container-based laundromat concept

### Mimo — apps + brand
- Online food delivery company, founded **2018** in Sarawak (Grab/foodpanda-style local play)
- Full delivery platform: Loyalty, Product Management, Order Management, Merchant Management, Rider Location Tracker, Rider HR Tools, Rider Slot Management, Rider Performance Reports, E-wallet
- Brand: orange rabbit-mark wordmark

### X.One — apps + IoT for an international brand
- X.One® is an internationally renowned, SGS-certified screen-protector brand
- **X-ONE Care** consumer app — loyalty, warranty tracking, ticket support, genuine-product authenticator, store locator, dealer management, issue tracker
- **X-ONE Xpress** — "most advanced screen protector film cutter in the market" — connected hardware machine with IoT, dealer management, wallet, analysis, issue tracking

### Favourite Livin — online marketing
- Interior renovation / space-planning client
- Full online marketing stack: social-media management, design services, Chinese-language video marketing, landing page management, online ads, budget allocation
- Landing page: "Renovation That Fits Your Lifestyle" — pain-point framing ("3 Big Headaches"), proof points (4200+ clients), CTAs (Book Now, Request callback). Strong direct-response template.
