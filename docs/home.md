## 3 · The problem

**Section eyebrow:** The gap we built Design Space to close

**Headline**
Most agencies stop at the website.

**Lead paragraph**
The brand looks good. The site launches. The invoice clears. And then your business is on its own — to figure out the CRM, the automations, the app, the data and the AI layer that actually compound growth. Twelve years in, we've watched too many good businesses get stuck at exactly that point. So we built Design Space to keep going.

**Four pain points** (icon + headline + one-liner)

1. **Disconnected vendors.** One shop for branding. Another for the site. Another for automation. Another for the app. Nothing talks to anything.
2. **One-off projects, zero compounding.** Every engagement starts from zero. Last year's work doesn't accelerate this year's.
3. **Not AI-ready.** The site, the data and the workflows weren't built for agents, automation or AI search. Retrofitting costs more than building it right.
4. **No system underneath.** There's a website — but no business system. Leads leak, data sits in spreadsheets, ops run on WhatsApp.

## 4 · The Design Space Digital Loop

**Section eyebrow:** How we think · How we build

**Headline**
One studio. Ten connected layers.

**Subheadline**
Brand → Website → Leads → CRM → Automation → App → IoT → Data → AI → Growth.

**Philosophy paragraph**
The Digital Loop is the operating model we work in. Every project plugs into the same system — brand work feeds the website, the website captures leads into the CRM, the CRM triggers automations, the automations power the app, the app generates data, the data trains the AI, and the AI compounds growth back into the brand. Most agencies own one slice. We own the loop.

## 5 · What we build

**Section eyebrow:** Seven pillars

**Headline**
Everything a modern digital business needs — under one roof.

**7 cards** (grid; pillar name · 1-line description · `Learn more →` link)

1. **Brand Launch** — Identity, positioning and brand systems built to scale across every layer below.
2. **AI-Ready Website** — Fast, accessible and structured for AI search and agents. Not a brochure — a system entry point.
3. **Business Automation** — Workflows and integrations across CRM, billing, comms and reporting. Ops that run themselves.
4. **Custom Web & Mobile App** — Booking, membership, dealer, loyalty, payment, portals and dashboards — on a reusable feature library.
5. **IoT** — Sensors, devices and gateways connected to the cloud, with dashboards and alerts that close the loop.
6. **Data Intelligence** — Pipelines, warehouses, dashboards and monthly insight reports. Your data, decision-ready.
7. **AI & Deep Learning Lab** — Agents, vision, language and predictive models — production-grade, not demos.
